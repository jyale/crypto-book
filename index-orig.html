Welcome to Crypto-Book!

Send people Facebook messages without letting Mark Zuckerberg snoop on you!

What do I do?

First you have to setup:

Download the desktop app
Run "java CreateMyKey" to generate a personal crypto-book.com link and a secret file.
Post that link to you Facebook (on the About Me page in the "websites" section, make it public or visible to friends).

Message friends
Decrypt messages with "java Decrypter"
